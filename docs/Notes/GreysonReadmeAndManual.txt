Fiasco User's Manual

Fiasco is a top-down shoot-em-up-ish game that has you stranded on a dangerous island. Will you destroy the cursed torch in the belly of Fiasco's volcano, or will you perish like those before you?

Desktop and Web Users:
Launch Fiasco.exe, Fiasco.app, or navigate to a webpage hosting Fiasco.
WASD to move
Mouse to aim
Left mouse button to shoot
P to pause the game and reveal the pause menu

Android Users:
Install Fiasco.apk (enabling installation of apks from outside sources in Settings if needed), and launch the app.
Tilt your phone to move
Tap your screen to shoot in that direction
Press the pause button to pause the game and reveal the pause menu
